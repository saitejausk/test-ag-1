FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project. Don't see your question? Call us at (513) 673-6986.

How long does the entire process take? +
From your first consultation to completed installation, most projects are finished in about 2 weeks. This includes measurement, slab selection, fabrication, and installation. Rush jobs may be accommodated — just ask.
Do you offer free estimates? +
Yes! All estimates are completely free with no obligation. We'll come to your home to take precise measurements and give you an accurate quote based on your chosen material and edge profile.
What materials do you carry? +
We work with granite, marble, quartzite, engineered quartz, soapstone, and porcelain. We source slabs from top suppliers and can help you find the perfect stone within your budget.
How do I care for my new countertop? +
Natural stone should be sealed once a year with a penetrating sealer. For daily care, use a mild dish soap and warm water — avoid acidic cleaners like vinegar or lemon juice. We provide full care instructions at installation.
What edge profiles are available? +
We offer eased, beveled, bullnose, ogee, mitered, and many custom profiles. Standard edges are included in your quote — some decorative profiles may have a small upcharge. We'll show you samples during your consultation.
Do you handle sink and faucet cutouts? +
Absolutely. We cut precise openings for undermount and drop-in sinks, faucet holes, soap dispensers, and more. Just bring your sink specs and we'll handle the rest.
Do you remove old countertops? +
Yes, we offer countertop removal and disposal as part of our installation service. Ask about this when you request your estimate.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve Dayton and the Greater Miami Valley including Kettering, Centerville, Beavercreek, Springboro, Miamisburg, Fairborn, Huber Heights, Troy, West Chester, Mason, and more. Call us if you're unsure — we cover a wide radius.
